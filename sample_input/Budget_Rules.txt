Budget planning     should begin with a realistic understanding of revenue, operating expenses, and reserve obligations...


Departments   must submit forecasts with clear assumptions, supporting    line items, and a summary of expected business impact!!!    

Reviews should compare proposed allocations against last quarter's spending pattern and current organizational priorities.


If exceptional spending is requested, the proposal should explain urgency, dependency risks, and measurable outcomes@@@
Leadership review should document approvals, deferrals, or requests for revision so the budget trail stays transparent and easy to audit.
